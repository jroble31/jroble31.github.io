Reader Tips

Whether you're trying to read more or find your next favorite book, these tips can help you stay inspired!

📚 Getting Out of a Reading Slump

Try re-reading an old favorite or switching genres! Sometimes a light, cozy book can rekindle your love for reading.

🗂️ Staying Organized

Use reading journals or apps like Notion or Goodreads to track your progress and reviews.

🎯 Choosing What to Read Next

Pick based on mood! Mix up genres and lengths so reading never feels like a chore.

📖 Challenge Your TBR

Set a monthly reading challenge for your physical books — even just one or two at a time!
